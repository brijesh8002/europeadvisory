Croatia Work Visa – Complete Guide

Croatia joined the EU in 2013 and the Schengen Area, making it an increasingly attractive destination. The tourism and healthcare sectors are particularly in need of foreign workers.

In-Demand Jobs in Croatia

  • Healthcare Workers
  • Tourism & Hospitality
  • Education Professionals
  • Construction Workers
  • IT Specialists

Average Salary Range: €15,000 – €45,000+

Key Requirements

  • Valid passport
  • Work permit approval from Croatian Employment Service
  • Employment contract
  • Proof of qualifications
  • Health insurance
  • Police clearance

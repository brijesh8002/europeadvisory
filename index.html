FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply for a European work visa without a job offer? ▼
Some countries like Germany and Ireland offer Job Seeker Visas, but most require employer sponsorship. Our team can help identify the best route for your situation.
How long does it take to process a European work visa? ▼
It varies by country but usually takes 4-12 weeks. Germany may take 8-12 weeks, while Malta and Croatia can be faster at 4-8 weeks.
Can you help me find a job in Europe? ▼
Yes! We assist with job searching, CV writing, and employer connections. We have partnerships with European companies actively seeking foreign workers.
What documents do I need for a European work visa? ▼
Most countries require: a valid passport, a job offer letter (if applicable), proof of qualifications, health insurance coverage, passport-size photos, and bank statements showing financial stability.
What is the cost of your immigration services? ▼
Our fees are transparent and competitive. We offer a free initial consultation. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your specific visa requirements.
Can my family join me in Europe? ▼
Yes! Most European work visas allow family reunification. Once you are settled with a valid work permit, your spouse and dependent children can typically apply for family visas.
